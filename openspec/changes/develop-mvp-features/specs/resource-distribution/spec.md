## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Relief Resource Distribution Platform
The platform SHALL provide a comprehensive resource distribution system that efficiently matches available resources with community needs during flood recovery operations.

#### Scenario: Real-time resource needs assessment
- **WHEN** community coordinators identify resource needs
- **THEN** they can submit detailed needs assessments including item categories, quantities, urgency levels, delivery locations, and beneficiary information
- **AND** system provides needs prioritization based on vulnerability assessments and recovery phases

#### Scenario: Resource donation and inventory management
- **WHEN** donors contribute relief resources
- **THEN** they can register donations with detailed descriptions, quantities, quality conditions, and availability schedules
- **AND** system maintains real-time inventory tracking with expiration dates and storage requirements

#### Scenario: Intelligent resource matching and allocation
- **WHEN** resources are available for distribution
- **THEN** system automatically matches donations with verified needs based on location proximity, urgency, and beneficiary requirements
- **AND** optimizes distribution routes and schedules for maximum efficiency and equity

#### Scenario: Distribution tracking and accountability
- **WHEN** resources are distributed to beneficiaries
- **THEN** system maintains complete audit trails including delivery confirmations, recipient acknowledgments, and impact assessments
- **AND** provides real-time visibility to donors and stakeholders

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Multi-Resource Category Support
The platform SHALL support multiple categories of relief resources with specialized handling and distribution workflows.

#### Scenario: Emergency supplies distribution
- **WHEN** distributing emergency supplies (food, water, medicine, shelter materials)
- **THEN** system tracks expiration dates, storage requirements, and regulatory compliance
- **AND** prioritizes distribution based on medical needs and vulnerability assessments

#### Scenario: Reconstruction materials allocation
- **WHEN** distributing construction and repair materials
- **THEN** system manages specialized delivery requirements, storage logistics, and contractor coordination
- **AND** integrates with repair services marketplace for just-in-time material delivery

#### Scenario: Volunteer and expertise matching
- **WHEN** coordinating volunteer resources
- **THEN** system matches volunteer skills and availability with specific community needs
- **AND** manages schedules, assignments, and safety considerations
