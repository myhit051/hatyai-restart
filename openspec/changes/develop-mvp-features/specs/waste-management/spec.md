## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Large-Scale Waste Management System
The platform SHALL provide a comprehensive waste management system for reporting, tracking, and coordinating large-scale waste removal and recycling operations.

#### Scenario: Waste reporting with detailed classification
- **WHEN** community members report large waste accumulations
- **THEN** they can submit detailed reports with location, waste type (hazardous, recyclable, organic, debris), estimated volume, and photos
- **AND** system automatically prioritizes reports based on environmental risk and public health impact

#### Scenario: Interactive waste mapping
- **WHEN** users view the waste management dashboard
- **THEN** they see an interactive map displaying all reported waste locations
- **AND** can filter by waste type, collection status, and urgency level
- **AND** access real-time updates on collection operations

#### Scenario: Resource allocation and scheduling
- **WHEN** waste collection teams operate in the field
- **THEN** they receive optimized routes and schedules based on waste locations and priorities
- **AND** can update collection status and capacity constraints in real-time

## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Waste Reporting System
The application SHALL enable comprehensive waste reporting, tracking, and coordination for large-scale debris removal and recycling operations.

#### Scenario: Comprehensive waste reporting
- **WHEN** users report waste locations
- **THEN** collect detailed location coordinates, waste classification (construction debris, hazardous materials, electronic waste, organic waste), estimated volume, accessibility information, and photographic evidence
- **AND** provide environmental risk assessment and public health impact ratings
- **AND** suggest appropriate disposal or recycling methods

#### Scenario: Advanced waste report display
- **WHEN** coordinators view reported waste locations
- **THEN** show location markers with comprehensive waste information including volume estimates, accessibility constraints, equipment requirements, and environmental risk ratings
- **AND** provide advanced filtering by waste type, collection priority, equipment needs, and environmental impact
- **AND** display collection team assignments and schedules
